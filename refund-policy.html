Refund Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

On This Page

Your satisfaction matters to us. If something isn't right with your order, here's how our return and refund process works.

1. Return Eligibility

Items are eligible for return within 7 days of delivery if they meet the following criteria:

  • ✓ Unworn, unwashed, and undamaged
  • ✓ Original tags and packaging intact
  • ✓ Proof of purchase (order ID)
  • ✗ Not a final sale or intimate wear item

2. Return Process

  1. Go to Dashboard → Order History and select the item to return
  2. Choose your reason for return and submit the request
  3. Our courier partner will schedule a free pickup within 2–3 business days
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed

3. Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The amount is credited to your original payment method (or store credit, if you choose). COD orders are refunded via bank transfer or UPI.

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded:

  • Items marked "Final Sale"
  • Innerwear, swimwear, and intimate apparel (for hygiene reasons)
  • Accessories with broken hygiene seals
  • Gift cards

5. Exchanges

Need a different size or color? You can request an exchange instead of a refund from the same Order History page — subject to stock availability. Exchange requests follow the same 7-day window and eligibility criteria.
