At KWĒN, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing address
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe/Razorpay — we never store card details)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Account credentials (encrypted)
  • Device and browser information collected automatically via cookies

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and support communications
  • Personalize your shopping experience and product recommendations
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Send marketing communications (only with your consent, and you can opt out anytime)
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted third parties necessary to operate our business: payment processors (Stripe, Razorpay), shipping partners (Shiprocket, Delhivery, Blue Dart, DHL), and email service providers (Resend) — all bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure cookies, and regular security audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@kwen.com or visit our Contact page.
