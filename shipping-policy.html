Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

On This Page

Delivery Timelines Shipping Charges Coverage Areas Order Tracking Delays & Exceptions

We work with trusted logistics partners to get your KWĒN order to you as quickly and safely as possible. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process.

1. Delivery Timelines

Delivery Type Estimated Time Cost
Standard Delivery 4–6 business days Free above ₹999
Express Delivery 1–2 business days ₹149
Same Day Delivery Same day (order before 12 PM) ₹299

2. Shipping Charges

Standard shipping is free for orders above ₹999. Orders below this threshold incur a flat shipping fee of ₹99. Express and Same Day options carry additional charges as listed above, shown clearly at checkout before payment.

3. Coverage Areas

We currently ship to all serviceable pin codes across India via our courier partners Shiprocket, Delhivery, Blue Dart, and DHL. Same Day Delivery is available only in select metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata).

4. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your order anytime from our Track Order page or your Dashboard.

5. Delays & Exceptions

While we strive to meet all delivery estimates, factors like weather, regional restrictions, or high-demand periods (sales, festivals) may cause delays. We'll notify you proactively if your order is affected.
